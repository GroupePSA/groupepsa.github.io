### `Device.GetHMITheme()`

| **Description** | Get the theme of the HMI
| **Response** | *Number* The number corresponding to the theme
| **Parameter**   | *Void*

#### Example

```javascript
var currentTheme = Device.GetHMITheme()
```

*Appeared in Software version 30.11.04.10*
